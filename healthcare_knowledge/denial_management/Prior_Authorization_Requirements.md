# Lakeside Health System — Prior Authorization Requirements Matrix

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Revenue Cycle Department**  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive guide to prior authorization requirements by payer, service, and diagnosis  
**Impact:** Prior Auth Required is the **#1 denial reason** at Lakeside, accounting for ~30% of all claim denials

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Prior authorization denials cost Lakeside Health System an estimated **$4.2M annually** in delayed/lost revenue and administrative rework. This matrix defines when PA is required, how to obtain it efficiently, and how to prevent the most common denial: **"Prior Auth Required."**

### 1.1 Key Statistics
- **Prior Auth Required** denial rate (system-wide): 12% of submitted claims
- Average PA processing time: 5-15 business days
- PA submission with complete documentation: **92% approval rate**
- PA submission with incomplete documentation: **45% approval rate**
- Each denied PA costs approximately **$118** in administrative rework

## 4. Preventing "Prior Auth Required" Denials

### 4.1 Root Causes at Lakeside
| Root Cause | % of PA Denials | Prevention |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| PA not obtained before service | 45% | Real-time eligibility check at scheduling |
| PA obtained but expired | 15% | Calendar alerts for PA expiration dates |
| PA obtained for wrong code | 12% | Verify CPT at order entry |
| PA obtained but not linked to claim | 10% | PA number in claim submission workflow |
| Emergent service, retro PA missed | 10% | 48-hour retro PA submission protocol |
| PA no longer required (payer changed) | 8% | Monthly payer policy update review |

### 4.2 Prevention Workflow
```
Step 1: Order Entry → System checks PA requirement matrix (this document)
Step 2: If PA required → Auto-route to PA Coordinator
Step 3: PA Coordinator assembles documentation (2 business days)
Step 4: Submit electronically to payer portal (same day)
Step 5: Track response → If approved, attach PA number to scheduled procedure
Step 6: Day-before-service verification → Confirm PA is active and covers CPT
Step 7: Claim submission → Include PA authorization number
```

### 4.3 Key Performance Indicators
| KPI | Current | Target |
|-----|---------|--------|
| PA submission within 2 business days of order | 65% | 90% |
| PA approval rate (complete documentation) | 88% | 95% |
| Claims denied for PA Required | 12% of denials | < 5% |
| Average PA turnaround time | 8 business days | 5 business days |
| Retro PA submission within 48 hours (emergent) | 70% | 95% |
