# Lakeside Health System — Network Adequacy Standards

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Managed Care & Contracting Department**  
**Regulatory Basis:** CMS Network Adequacy (42 CFR 422.116), Michigan DIFS requirements  
**Applicable:** All payer contracts, all facilities (FAC001-FAC008)

---

## 1. Overview

Network adequacy ensures patients have reasonable access to providers and facilities within their insurance network. Inadequate networks lead to **Out of Network** denials (6% of Lakeside denials) and patient access barriers.

### 1.1 Regulatory Requirements
| Regulator | Standard | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| CMS (Medicare Advantage) | Time/distance standards by specialty and county | Star rating impact, marketing restrictions |
| Michigan DIFS | State insurance code compliance | Contract penalties, enrollment suspension |
| NCQA (Accreditation) | Provider-to-member ratios | Accreditation status at risk |

### 3.2 Network Gaps Identified
| Gap | Impact | Remediation Plan |
|-----|--------|-----------------|
| FAC002 not in Cigna (PAY004) network | Heart Center patients with Cigna denied as OON | Negotiate single-case agreements, pursue contract |
| FAC003 not in Aetna (PAY002) network | Traverse City UC patients limited | Contract negotiation in progress |
| FAC007 not in Aetna/Cigna/Tricare | Rehab patients redirected to competitors | Priority contract negotiation |
| FAC008 not in UHC/Medicaid/Priority | Ohio campus limited to certain payers | Different state requires separate contracts |

### 3.3 No Surprises Act Compliance (Federal)
- **Emergency services:** ALL facilities MUST treat regardless of network status
- **Balance billing prohibited** for emergency services at OON facilities
- **Good faith estimates** required for uninsured/self-pay (PAY012) patients
- **Independent dispute resolution (IDR)** for OON payment disputes

## 6. Access Standards

### 6.1 Appointment Availability Standards
| Appointment Type | Standard | Measurement |
|-----------------|---------|-------------|
| Routine primary care | Within 7 business days | Scheduling system audit |
| Urgent care (non-emergency) | Same day or next business day | Scheduling + UC walk-in data |
| Specialist referral (routine) | Within 14 business days | Referral tracking |
| Specialist referral (urgent) | Within 3 business days | Referral tracking |
| Mental health (routine) | Within 10 business days | Scheduling audit |
| Mental health (urgent) | Within 48 hours | Crisis line + scheduling |
| Emergency department | Immediate (no scheduling) | ED metrics |
| Post-discharge follow-up | Within 7 days of discharge | Care coordination tracking |
