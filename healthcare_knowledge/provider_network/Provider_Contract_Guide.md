### 2.3 DRG-Based Payment (Inpatient — Medicare Part A)
| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Base rate | CMS-published, adjusted for geographic wage index |
| DRG weight | Case-mix adjustment (severity) |
| Outlier payments | For extremely high-cost cases |
| Readmission adjustment | -1 to -3% for HRRP conditions |
| Quality adjustment | HAC, VBP adjustments |

### 2.4 Value-Based Payment Components
| Payer | VBP Model | Potential Bonus/Risk |
|-------|----------|---------------------|
| BCBS MI (PAY001) | Quality bonus pool | Up to 2% bonus for HEDIS performance |
| Aetna (PAY002) | Shared savings | Share savings if total cost below benchmark |
| UHC (PAY003) | Stars-based | Tiered bonus for quality measures |
| Medicare (PAY006/007) | CMS Stars + HRRP + VBP | 5% Stars bonus, -3% readmission penalty |
| Medicaid MI (PAY008) | Quality withhold | 2% revenue at-risk for quality targets |
| Priority Health (PAY009) | Direct incentives | Per-measure bonus payments |

## 5. Self-Pay and Charity Care (PAY012)

### 5.1 Self-Pay Discount Structure
| Income Level (% FPL) | Discount | Process |
|----------------------|---------|---------|
| < 200% FPL | 100% charity care | Financial counselor application |
| 200-300% FPL | 75% discount | Sliding scale application |
| 300-400% FPL | 50% discount | Sliding scale application |
| > 400% FPL | 25% prompt-pay discount | Offered at time of service |

### 5.2 No Surprises Act — Good Faith Estimates
- Required for ALL self-pay/uninsured patients
- Must be provided within **3 business days** of scheduling
- Must itemize expected charges by CPT code
- Patient can dispute if final bill exceeds estimate by > $400
